General Security Tips

Protecting Your Recovery Phrase

Your 12-word recovery phrase is the key to your Bitcoin wallet. Here are essential tips to keep it secure:

  • Write it down on paper and store it in a secure location
  • Never store it digitally (no photos, screenshots, or cloud storage)
  • Don't share it with anyone, not even Valet support
  • Consider using a metal backup solution for long-term storage
  • Keep multiple copies in different secure locations

Important: Anyone with access to your recovery phrase can access your Bitcoin. Treat it like the key to your physical wallet.

Device Security

Keep your device and wallet secure with these practices:

  • Set up a PIN or biometric authentication for your device
  • Keep your device's operating system updated
  • Install apps only from trusted sources
  • Use a secure internet connection when making transactions
  • Consider using a dedicated device for your Bitcoin wallet

Tip: Regularly backup your wallet and test the recovery process to ensure you can access your funds if needed.

Transaction Security

When making transactions, follow these security practices:

  • Double-check all addresses before sending Bitcoin
  • Start with small amounts when testing new features
  • Verify transaction details before confirming
  • Be cautious of phishing attempts and scams
  • Keep your backup files secure and encrypted

Remember: Bitcoin transactions are irreversible. Always verify the recipient's address and amount before sending.
