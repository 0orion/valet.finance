Restoring Your Valet Wallet

Restoring from Recovery Phrase

If you need to restore your Valet wallet on a new device or after reinstalling the app, you can do so using your 12-word recovery phrase. Here's how:

  1. Launch the Valet app on your device
  2. Click on Restore Wallet instead of creating a new one
  3. Enter your 12-word recovery phrase in the correct order
  4. Click Restore to complete the process

Tip: Make sure you enter the recovery phrase exactly as you wrote it down, including the correct order of words. Any mistake in the order or spelling will prevent the wallet from being restored.

Restoring from Backup File

If you have a backup file from your previous wallet, you can restore your wallet using that file. Here's how:

  1. Launch the Valet app on your device
  2. Click on Restore from Backup
  3. Select your backup file from the storage location
  4. Follow the on-screen instructions to complete the restoration

Note: The backup file contains sensitive information about your wallet. Make sure to keep it secure and never share it with anyone.
