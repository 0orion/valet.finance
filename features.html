What Can Valet Do For You?

Your Personal Bitcoin Wallet

Valet is like having your own personal bank in your pocket, but for Bitcoin. Here's what makes it special:

Complete Control Over Your Money

Unlike traditional banking apps, Valet gives you full control over your Bitcoin. Think of it as having your own personal vault where you're the only one with the key.

  • No one else can access your funds
  • No waiting for bank approvals
  • Send and receive money anytime, anywhere

Lightning-Fast Payments

Valet includes Lightning Network support, which is like having a super-fast payment system built into your wallet.

  • Pay for coffee in seconds
  • Send money to friends instantly
  • Lower fees than traditional Bitcoin transactions

Security Features

Your security is our top priority. Here's how Valet keeps your Bitcoin safe:

Backup and Recovery

Lose your phone? No problem! Valet provides a simple 12-word recovery phrase that works like a master key to your wallet.

  • Easy to write down and store safely
  • Recover your wallet on any device
  • Never lose access to your funds

Extra Security Layers

Add extra protection to your wallet with these security features:

  • PIN code protection
  • Fingerprint authentication (on supported devices)
  • Hardware wallet support for maximum security

Smart Money Management

Valet makes managing your Bitcoin easy and efficient:

Smart Transaction Features

Save money on fees and manage your transactions better:

  • Batch multiple payments together to save on fees
  • Choose which coins to spend from
  • Set custom transaction fees

Easy to Use Interface

Valet is designed to be user-friendly:

  • Clear balance display
  • Simple send and receive buttons
  • Transaction history at your fingertips

Real-World Use Cases

Here are some ways people use Valet in their daily lives:

For Daily Purchases

Use Valet to pay for your morning coffee or lunch using Lightning Network - it's faster than using a credit card!

For International Payments

Send money to family abroad without expensive wire transfer fees or long waiting times.

For Savings

Use Valet as your personal Bitcoin savings account, with complete control over your funds.

Multiple Currency Support
