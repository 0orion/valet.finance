How to install Speed Wallet?

Speed Bitcoin Lightning Wallet can be downloaded from the Google Play Store on Android and the App Store on iOS. Speed Wallet is also available on the Chrome Web Store on Desktop.

Why is Speed Wallet the best Bitcoin Wallet?

Speed Wallet is one of the fastest-growing and highly rated Bitcoin wallet. It is highly secure and offers instant Bitcoin transactions at low cost. You can create your personalized LN address to receive Bitcoins, and shop gift cards. Earn Speed points with every transaction and redeem them for Bitcoin rewards. Fast, easy, and rewarding - that's Speed Wallet for you!

Can I shop using Bitcoin through Speed Wallet?

Absolutely! With Speed Wallet, you can shop gift cards using Bitcoin seamlessly. We have integrated with Bitrefill, offering gift cards from popular brands like Amazon, Starbucks, Uber, and more. You can pay directly using the Bitcoins in your Speed Wallet.
